Preserving the Legacy of our ancestors.

Kerala is a narrow strip of land protected by natural boundaries. The uniqueness of the state is its cultural diversity. Our mission is to keep these traditions alive.

01 — Signature Venue

Koothambalam

Traditional dance theatre structure designed scientifically for acoustics and aesthetics. The wooden pillars and intricate carvings transport you to an era of royal performances.

02 — The Gateway

Padippura

The traditional arched gateway serves as a grand entrance and a smaller venue for intimate gatherings, photoshoots, and VIP receptions.

03 — Open Air

Kalmandapam

A granite floor performance space ideal for martial arts like Kalaripayattu. The open-air setting provides a natural ambiance for earthy performances.
