About Us

Kerala is a narrow strip of land on the South West coast of India protected by natural boundaries and blessed with scenic beauty. The uniqueness of the state is the cultural diversity and its vibrance. Due to these reasons, Kerala stands apart from other states. Our art forms range from classical, temple arts, rituals, folk and tribal art forms. This cultural tradition influenced our socio-political history of the state.

Due to the cultural stream rolling, cultural values and art forms are sink into obscurity and all these have to be preserved intact and passed on to the coming generations. Padippura

The principal gateway of the complex, Padippura welcomes the visitors initially. The Padippura resembles the gateway of Padmanabhapuram Palace.

Koothambalam

Natyasasthra (a Sanskrit treatise on performing arts) describes the structure of dance theatres scientifically and comprehensively.

Kalmandapam

This performance space has been constructed with granite floor and a tiled roof supported by four pillars. This Kalmandapam serves as a venue for traditional art forms.
